The Plenum represents the supreme authority of the Sámi Parliament. Sääʹmteeʹǧǧ sååbbar eeʹttkâʹstt Sääʹmteeʹǧǧ pââimõs tuʹmmjemvääʹld.
It meets 4-5 times per year. Tõt noorââtt 4-5 vuâra eeʹjjest.
The Plenum decides mainly on the statements, proposals, and other comments. Sääʹmteeʹǧǧ sååbbar tuʹmmai jäänmõsân nåkam ceâlklmõõžžin, čuäʹjtõõzzin da jeeʹres beäʹlestväʹlddmõõžžid, koid sääʹmteʹǧǧ lij ouddam.
The Council of State appoints the people who have gained the most votes in the elections as members (21) and deputies (4). However, every municipality of the Sámi homeland (Enontekiö, Inari, Sodankylä, and Utsjoki) must have at least three members and one deputy.
