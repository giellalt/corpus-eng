Numra

  • Options

Sámegillii | På norsk | In English | Suomeksi | På svenska

Numra is a program for practicing numerals. We recommend Numra to all students of Saami: In order to learn a language you must know its numeral system.

Options

  • Range: 0-10, 0-20, 0-100 or 0-1000.
  • Mode: From number to numeral, and from numeral to number.
  • Ordinal numbers.
  • Time
  • Dates
