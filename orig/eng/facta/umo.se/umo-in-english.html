English (engelska)

UMO.se is a website for young people in Sweden with information about sexuality, health and relationships. Here we have selected and translated some of UMO’s texts. Browse through these to get an idea of what UMO is all about.

These translated pages are within UMO:s Swedish framework and therefore the main menu and functions are in Swedish. To take a look at the start page of UMO, click here.

In the Female genital cutting sections, you will find a portlet called Dölj bilder. This means Hide pictures and hides images that depict nudity and sexual situations. We have developed this function to make the website accessible to people who for various reasons do not want to, or may not be allowed to, view such images.

If you want to contact UMO, you are welcome to e-mail us at info@umo.se.
