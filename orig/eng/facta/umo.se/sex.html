Sex

Sex

Sex can be all the various things that you do and feel when your body or thoughts are stimulated in a sexual way. There is no strict definition of what sex is and what it is not.  You decide for yourself based on your experiences and interests.

The word sex stems from the word sexuality. Sexuality brings many things to mind. One of these things can be what qualities you have as a person and which sex you identify yourself as. It can also raise thoughts of who you choose to have sex with, how and when you have sex and how your sexuality is regarded by others.
