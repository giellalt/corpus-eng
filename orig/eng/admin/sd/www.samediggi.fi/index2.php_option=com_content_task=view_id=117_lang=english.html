Architectural competition

Senate Properties is organising a general two-stage architectural competition for the design of the Sámi Cultural Centre.

 

Competition is open to all citizens from the European Unions States, and to citizens from those countries that have concluded the GP agreement with the European Union, according to each country´s currently valid agreements and laws. At least one of the member participating in the design team preparing the competition must be legally entitled to practise the profession of achitecture in his or her respective country.

 

1st stage is from 14 April to 30 June 2008.

2nd stage will begin 1 September and conclude 3 October 2008.
