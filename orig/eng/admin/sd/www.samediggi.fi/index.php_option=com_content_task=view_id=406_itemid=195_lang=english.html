Renting facilities

The Sámi Cultural Centre Sajos's premises are perfect for concerts, theatrical performances, exhibitions and fairs, and also for various special occasions. The Sámi Cultural Centre Sajos provides venues for meetings and conferences of different scale. All our facilities have modern conference technology and communications capabilities.
