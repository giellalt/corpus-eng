Conference room Silba (smaller)

The Silba conference (see room plan Nr 1) room is suitable for small meetings or group work. Silba is located on the first floor adjacent to the lounge, with views out over Lake Inari and highway 4. When connected to the larger Násti conference room, space is available for 25 persons (50 m2). The word silba is North Sámi and means silver. Silver is one of the raw materials used in Sámi handicraft for making jewellery, ornaments, etc.
