Other services

Saamelaiskulttuurikeskus Sajoksen asiakkaille on tarjolla monenlaista oheisohjelmaa kesällä ja talvella. Tässä on lueteltu muutama hyvä esimerkki Inarin laajasta ohjelmapalveluvalikoimast.

 

GUIDED TOUR AT SAMI CULTURAL CENTRE SAJOS

This guided tour of the Sajos Sámi Cultural Centre offers an introduction to the daily life and celebrations of the modern Sámi. Sámi crafts and music are especially valued here at Sajos. The tour will introduce how Sámi education, administration and culture go hand in hand with the everyday life and celebrations of the Sámi.

Duration: 1 h

Runs: all year round

Languages: Finnish, English, Deutch, Sámi

Price: 130 €/group of 1-25 persons.

 

 

GUIDED TOUR OF THE SÁMI MUSEUM SIIDA

The Sámi Museum and Nature Centre Siida are musts for any tourists visiting Inari. The Siida exhibitions portray Sámi history and culture and the nature of northernmost Lapland. The museum houses two permanent displays and a series of non-permanent exhibitions. Visiting groups will be given a fascinating introduction to the world of the Sámi at the hands of a knowledgeable local guide. Summer tours include a visit to the outdoor museum area. Siida is located about 300 metres from Sajos. Duration: 2 hours. Availability: Throughout the year. Tour languages: Finnish, English and German. Group size: max 25 persons/guide. The site is suitable for large groups. 

 

MAKE YOUR OWN SÁMI KEEPSAKE

At Sajos we value Sámi handicrafts. After this handicrafts course you will know what ‘sisna’ and ‘tiuhta’ mean and appreciate the reindeer as a source of material for a range of crafts. You even get to make your own piece of Sápmi to take home with you. The course takes place at Sajos.

Duration 1-2 hours

Runs: All year round

Group size: 10-40

 

SÁMI MUSIC CONCERT

The Sami chant, the yoik, traditionally had a dual function. The Sámi Cultural Centre´s auditorium is specially a concert hall.  Sajos organizes sámi music concerts for groups. The Sámi music is also suitable for cocktail events. Duration: 15 minutes – 1,5 hours. Price depends on the event and artist. We will make an offer!
