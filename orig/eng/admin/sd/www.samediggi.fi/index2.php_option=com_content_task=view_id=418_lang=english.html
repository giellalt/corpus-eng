Multipurpose Hall Dollagáddi

The Dollagáddi multipurpose hall is particularly suitable for exhibitions, fairs, dinners and lectures. The semi-circular hall is situated behind the stage of the Dolla auditorium, separated by a movable partition, and adjacent to the Galla restaurant. For catering purposes the hall is excellent for serving coffee to large groups, for lunches and evening meals, and for cocktail occasions. The word dollagáddi is North Sámi and refers to the apron around a camp fire.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

CAPACITY AND ROOM SIZE:

* Capacity for 70-140 people 

* Total area: 116,5 m2 (width  16 m x depth 8,5 m)

 

* Class format: 70 people
* Diplomat format: 44 people
* U format: 44 people
* Theatre format: 140 people

 

TECHNOLOGY AND EQUIPMENT

* Data projector 1280 x 800

* Document camera

* Video conferencing deck

* Blu-ray player, DVD/VHS combination player

* WLAN

* Sound system

* Size of the whiteboard:

* Flip chart

* Lectern

 

AVAILABLE AT EXTRA COST:

* Conference catering service.

* Recording facilities

 

OTHER POINTS TO NOTE:

* Licence to serve alcohol in the Main Hall. Catering services must be ordered in advance.

* Access for maintenance (door width 2.2 m) leads directly on the the stage in the Main Hall.
