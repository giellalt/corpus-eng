Sat 24.10.2015 at 5 pm
BEAIVVÁŠ SÁMI NAŠUNÁLATEÁHTER "SKOAVDNJI"
The play "Skoavdnji" addresses both the good and evil sides of human nature. The original source of the play was a ghost story from the eastern coast of Finnmark, dramatized and re-written by the Sami playwright Inger Margrethe Olsen (1956-2010). The setting of the play is in a coastal Sami environment in the 1920’s. The recommended age limit is 14 years. Read more
