Parliament Hall Solju

Solju, the Parliament Hall, is a conference room furnished to the highest standards. The hall is the venue for the general assembly of the Sámi Parliament and for meetings of the Board, but may also be hired as for conferences. The hall name Solju is North Sámi and means risku, a traditional item of Sámi jewellery.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

ROOM SIZE AND CAPACITY:

* 60 persons; 23 seats around the circular parliament table, 12 seats at six desk-type tables and 25 theatre-type seats (i.e. without tables). See floor plan at bottom of page.

* Tables and chairs can be arranged in a variety of formats appropriate to the occasion.

* A floor area 125 m2 and a height in excess of 10 metres. The hall is oval in shape; maximum length is approx. 15 m and maximum width approx. 10 m.

 

TECHNOLOGY AND EQUIPMENT IN THE PARLIAMENT HALL SOLJU

* Data projector 1280 x 800

* Document camera

* Video conferencing deck

* Wireless conference system (sound recording facilities and voting)

* Recorded and simultaneous interpretation in four languages (four permanent interpreter booths)
* Four fully equipped interpreter booths, interpreter booth sizes: 3 booths x 5 m2 and 1 booth x 7.5m2 *

* Blu-ray player, DVD/VHS combination player

* WLAN

* Sound system

* Size of electronic whiteboard: 5 m x 5 m

* Flip chart

*

BY SEPARATE ARRANGEMENT/ADDITIONAL FEE:

* Wireless conference system (sound recording facilities)

* Recorded and simultaneous interpretation in four languages (four permanent interpreter booths)

* Conference catering service

 

OTHER POINTS TO NOTE:

* Lisence to serve alcohol in the Parliament Hall. Catering services must be ordered in

advance.
