Restaurant Lyeme

The Lyeme Restaurant accommodates 50 guests, which can be increased to 130–150 by making used of the adjoining Dollagáddi Hall. The restaurant is situated on the first floor next to the lobby and conference rooms. The large windows of the restaurant offer magnificent views of the Juutuanjoki river and partly of Lake Inari. The Lyeme is a licensed restaurant.
