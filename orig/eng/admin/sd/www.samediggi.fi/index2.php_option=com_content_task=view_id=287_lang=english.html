Anna Näkkäläjärvi as deputy for the Cultural Secretary
Master of Music Anna Näkkäläjärvi has been appointed as deputy for the Cultural Secretary. Näkkäläjärvi will begin her work on 18 May in the Saami Parliaments General office in Inari.

 

Further information from Executive Director Juha Guttorm.
