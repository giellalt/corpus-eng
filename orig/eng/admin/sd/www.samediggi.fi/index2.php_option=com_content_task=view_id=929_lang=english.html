Accessibility

All the conference and public space is accessible by wheelchair. The main doors open automatically and there are no high thresholds. Corridors and door openings are wide.

We have reserved space in all of the concerts and other events for people moving with a wheelchair. People with mobility problems may enter the space in ample time before the beginning of an event.

The assistants are welcome free of charge to the own productions of Sajos. However, always inquire about the matter when reserving tickets as outside productions have their own policies.

Induction Loop

A fixed induction loop system is available for hearing aid users in the main auditorium and the parliament hall Solju.

Parking

Disabled parking places are clearly marked with signs. From the parking lot there is a paved path leading to the main doors.

Toilets

Accessible toilets are available in the ground floor.
