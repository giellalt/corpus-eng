Cultural Committee

The Cultural Committee is a body under the Sámi Parliament Executive Board. It prepares the proposals, initiatives and statements for the Sámi Parliament that concern the spiritual culture of the Sámi.  The purpose of the Cultural Committee is to decide how to distribute the government subsidy (Sámi Culture Allowance) and to monitor and supervise the use of granted subsidies.  The committee prepares an annual review of the use of the culture allowance and plans a suggestion for the national budget on the Sámi Culture Allowance.

 

The Cultural Secretary functions as a preparer and representative of Cultural Committee issues.
