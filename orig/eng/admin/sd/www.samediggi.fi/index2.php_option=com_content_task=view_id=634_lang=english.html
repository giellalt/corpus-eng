Ijahis idja- festival grew again
Ijahis idja- music festival of the indigenous people was held on 17 – 19 August 2012 around the newly opened Sámi Cultural Centre Sajos in Inari.

Two day festival was organized already for the ninth time and attracted around 2200 visitors over the two days. Festival was successful and Sajos served well as a festival venue.

 

The most popular event of the festival was the saturday´s concert, which main artists were the Sámi popsinger Sofia Jannok and the rock band from Greenland called Nanook.

 

Ijahis idja! - consert was also performed in YLE Sápmi live broadcast in the internet. Broadcast was extremely popular and people around the world attended to Ijahis idja- festival via broadcast.
