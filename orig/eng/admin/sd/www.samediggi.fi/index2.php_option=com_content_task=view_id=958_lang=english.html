Sámi handicraft flea market and Sámi handicraft market at Sajos 12th 13th of December 2014
Sámi Duodji association organizes in cooperation with the Youth Council of Sámi Parliament and the Sámi Cultural Centre Sajos Sámi handicraft flea market and market at Sajos, Inari 12th – 13th of December 2014.

 

Sámi handicraft flea market on Friday 12th of December at 12-17.

 

Come to the flea market for bargains! Sámi traditional dress (gákti), brooch (risku), earrings or other handicraft that is no longer needed for someone, can be a treasure for you. You can also find Sámi handicraft materials such as fabrics, threads, silk, skins and so on. At the flea market there is also a group table if you don’t have enough stuff for your own table. During the flea market performs Tiina Aikio from Vuotso and her accompanist Tony Brandtberg at 2 pm.

 

Remember, that one man's trash is another man's treasure and retro is in right now!

 

Psst, the 4th Club night of Sajos starts at 8 pm.

 

 

Sámi handicraft market on Saturday 13th of December at 10–17

 

Saturday is the market day and sellers all around the Sámi land gather to the lobby of Sajos. Markets are a brilliant place to explore the latest trends of Sámi handicrafts as well as make Christmas shopping.

 

During the market day at 1 pm performs a group called Ravggon, they play folk-rock music in Northern Sámi.

 

Also kids are very welcome to the markets! Sámi children’s center Mánnu organizes a handicraft workshop at 10-12.

 

When you get hungry, Restaurant Lyeme offers market food for smaller and bigger hunger.

 

 

There is still time to register as a seller to the markets!
