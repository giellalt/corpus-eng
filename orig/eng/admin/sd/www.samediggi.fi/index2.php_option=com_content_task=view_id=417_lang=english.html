Conference room Silbanásti (connected)

This conference room Silbanásti is suitable for meetings and for group works. Silbanásti is connected with the conference room Silba (Nr 1)  and conference room Násti (Nr 2). The conference room Silbanásti (Nr 1+2) is located on the first floor. A view over the Lake Inarijärvi, the River Juutuanjoki and the main road.

 

 

 

ROOM SIZE AND CAPACITY:  

* Area: 50 m2 (depth 9 m x width 5,5 m)

* Class format: 25 people

* Diplomat format: 25 people

* U format: 25 people

* Theatre format: 30 people

 

TECHNOLOGY AND EQUIPMENT:

* Data projector 2500, 16:9

* Blu-ray player, DVD/VHS combination player

* WLAN

* Sound system

* Size of electronic whiteboard: 5 m x 12.5 m

* Flip chart

 

AVAILABLE AT EXTRA COST:

* Conference catering service.

 

OTHER POINTS TO NOTE:

* Licence to serve alcohol in the Silbanásti. Catering services must be ordered in advance.
