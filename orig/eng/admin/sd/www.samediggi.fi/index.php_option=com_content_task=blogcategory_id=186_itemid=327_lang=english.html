Auditorium Dolla

The auditorioum Dolla, with a banked auditorioum and facilities for simultaneous translation in five languages. Dolla as a concert and conference hall of Sajos is the largest and the most modern auditorium in the Northern Lapland.

 

The word dolla is North Sámi and means fire. Dolla auditorium can be adapted to Basic, Seminar and Mini formats depending on the nature of the event.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

THE BASIC FORMAT AUDITORIUM for 255 persons

 

ROOM SIZE AND CAPACITY:

* Auditorium: fixed seating in a banked auditorium for 254 people (12 rows) and 1 seat for lecture. 

* Stage: 84 m2 (depth 4,5 m x width 17 m)

* Total area 298 m2 (depth 18,5m x width 17 m)

 

THE SEMINAR FORMED AUDITORIUM for 310 persons

ROOM SIZE AND CAPACITY:

* Auditorium: fixed seating in a banked auditorium for 190 people (9 rows) + temporary seating in the central floor area for 116 (4 rows) and 4 seats for lecturers. Overall seating for 310 persons.

* Stage: 43 m2 (width 17 m x depth 2,5 m) 

* Total area: 298 m2 (depth 18,5 m x width 17 m)

 

THE MINI FORMED AUDITORIUM for 195 persons

 

 

ROOM SIZE AND CAPACITY:

* Auditorium: fixed seating for 190 persons (9 rows) and 5 seats for lecturers 

* Stage: 136 m2 (width 17 m x depth 8 m) 

* Total area 298 m2 (depth 18,5 m x width 17 m)

 

TECHNOLOGY AND EQUIPMENT IN DOLLA AUDITORIUM/MAIN HALL:

* Data projector 1920 x 1200 / WXGA 700

* Document camera

* Video conferencing deck

* Blu-ray player, DVD/VHS combination player

* WLAN

* Sound system

* Size of electronic whiteboard: 5 m x 12.5 m

* Flip chart

* Lectern

* Five fully equipped interpreter booths (2 x 9,5 m2, 2 x 6 m2 and 1 x 8 m2)

 

AVAILABLE AT EXTRA COST:

* Recorded and simultaneous interpretation in five languages (five permanent interpreter booths)

* Interpreter booth sizes: 2 booths x 9.5 m2, 2 booths x 6 m2 and 1 booth x 8 m2

* Stage dance floor

* Conference catering service

* Recording facilities

 

OTHER POINTS TO NOTE:

* License to serve alcohol in the Main Hall. Catering services must be ordered in advance.

* Access for maintenance (door width 2.2 m) leads directly on to the stage in the Main Hall. Small vehicles may be used to bring equipment etc. into the hall.
