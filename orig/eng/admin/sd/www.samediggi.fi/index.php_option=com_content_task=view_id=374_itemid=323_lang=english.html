Restaurant Lyeme

The Lyeme Restaurant accommodates 50 guests, which can be increased to 130–150 by making used of the adjoining Dollagáddi Hall. The restaurant is situated on the first floor next to the lobby and conference rooms. The large windows of the restaurant offer magnificent views of the Juutuanjoki river and partly of Lake Inari. The Lyeme is a licensed restaurant.

RESTAURANT


Request an offer for dinners, celebration events, cocktail events, etc. Restaurant Lyeme offers plenty of options to customize the event. Menu is designed to meet the nature of the event as well as customer’s needs. Served at the restaurant, conference rooms or lounge. 



OTHER SERVICES


Restaurant Lyeme serves also coffee and pastries either on the terrace or indoors. Lyeme will also organize weddings and other family celebrations, get-togethers and catering services.



OPENING TIMES:


•Restaurant Lyeme is open on weekdays, from Monday to Friday from 9 a.m. - 4 p.m., Sat-Sun closed.

•The restaurant is open at other times, for example during conferences and events, by separate arrangement.

•Lunch buffet is available on weekdays Mon-Fri from 11 a.m.-3 p.m., at other times as agreed for groups (group minimum 20 people).
