Sámi Giellagáldu  The Nordic Resource Center for Saami Languages
The Saami Parliamentary Council (the cooperative organ of the Saami parliaments in Finland, Sweden and Norway) has launched 1st of January 2013 an establishment project of the Nordic Resource Center for Saami languages. During the project the cooperation of Saami languages will be developed and strengthened by establishing a common Saami Language Center, Sámi Giallagáldu. The object of Sámi Giellagáldu is to gather professional language services to help finding information. It is intended that the language center has the highest responsibility for the Saami language cooperation, the professional language work and Saami language services aimed for language users.
  
The center consists of five different Saami languages; South-, Lule-, North-, Inari-, and Skolt Saami language workers and language divisions. These operators will meet to discuss the Saami language issues so that the cooperation between Saami languages increases and strengthens. During the project the operations of the language center will be coordinated and connected to the responsibilities, operations and financial resources of the existing institutions. In addition, during the project the three Saami Parliaments will decide how the language center will be funded permanently and establish permanent language center when the project ends in 2014. Giellagáldu was established in Inari 20th of February 2013, and all together nine language workers started working in the project.

Objectives of the project
 
Sámi Giellagáldu is a body operating under the Saami Parliamentary Council and it is the shared decision-making body of Saami people in Saami language issues. The goal of the project is to preserve and promote the culture heritage belonging to the Saami language and thus to act as a professional body of experts, to combine and coordinate the cooperation of Saami languages.
 
Sámi Giellagáldu is responsible together with Saami Parliaments:
• strengthening and developing the Nordic language cooperation
• strengthening and developing  Saami language so that the individual needs, challenges and resources of each language group are taken into account, and
• revitalizing and securing Saami language in the future
 
The task of Nordic Saami Language Center is to monitor all of the Saami language issues in cooperation with the Saami Parliaments and other Saami language sector institutions. The center also keeps contact to other institutions throughout the Saami region and internationally. Tasks and responsibilities of the center are dynamic so that the operation can be developed to respond the current situation and needs of the Saami professional language. 
 
At first the language center is not established as a physical institution, but it operates in connection of each country’s Saami Parliament. During the project the language center will be established as a permanent center and its funding and operational model will be ensured. The objects of the project together with Saami Parliaments are to strengthen and develop Saami language so that each language group's individual needs, challenges and resources are taken into account and the revitalization and securing Saami language in the future. 
 
Tasks of Sámi Giellagáldu
 
Center’s tasks include language planning and development, terminology work, standardization and addressing of place name issues. The employees of Sámi Giellagáldu are responsible of practical language work and they prepare issues related to their own language for language divisions for decision making. 
The language divisions will provide recommendations on matters related to Saami languages. 
The language workers will also provide guidance and advices on matters related to the use of Saami language.
