Solution of the architectural competition

The Competition Jury decided to award 1st Prize to Entry No. 37 “ada”, 2nd Prize to No. 28 “Salvu” and 3rd Prize to No. 51 “Villa Sapmi” as well as a Purchase to No. 33 “Varpu”. Honourable Mentions were awarded to No. 19 “Liike” and No. 32 “Baiki”.
