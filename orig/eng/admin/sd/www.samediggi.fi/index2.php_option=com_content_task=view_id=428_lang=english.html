Lounge

The Lounge is situated in the heart of the Sámi Cultural Centre Sajos. For meeting purposes the lounge is suitable for breaks. It is also suitable for small fairs and exhibitions. The lounge is located on the first floor adjacent to restaurant, library, Duodji Shop, information and meeting rooms, with views out over the River Juutuanjoki, Lake Inari and highway 4.
