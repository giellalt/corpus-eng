SAT 6.2.2016 
THE SÁMI NATIONAL DAY EVENTS AT SAJOS

Duodji shop open 10 a.m. – 5 p.m., Sámi library open 10 a.m. – 4 p.m.

At 2 p.m. MU DOHKKA – Exhibition opening

The Sámi Children’s Cultural Centre opens exhibition MY DOHKKA, with the theme dolls and toys. The purpose is to show a variety of hand-made dolls, as well as the simplicity and ecological thinking of toys. The exhibition is organized in co-operation with Sámi Duodji Ry and Kautokeino Duodji Institute. The exhibition will be on display in Sajos from 6th of February to 31st of May 2016.

At 2.30 p.m. Children’s musical moment for the little ones/ Anna Näkkäläjärvi-Länsman

Sat 19.11.2016
SÁMI HANDICRAFT MARKET AND FLEA MARKET

Sámi Duodji association organizes in cooperation with Sámi Cultural Centre Sajos Sámi handicraft market and flea market at Sajos, Inari 19th of November 2016. Sellers all around the Sámi land gather to the lobby of Sajos. The market is a great place to explore the latest trends of Sámi handicrafts and make Christmas gift shopping.
