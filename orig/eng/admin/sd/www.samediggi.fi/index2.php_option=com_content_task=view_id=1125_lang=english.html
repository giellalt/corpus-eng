Famous Sámi Musicians in Sajos During Kaamosjazz Festival
Famous traditional Sámi artists Hilda Länsman and Wimme Saari will perform in Sámi Cultural Center Sajos auditorium in Inari on Saturday 21.11.2015.


Joik artist Wimme Saari has become world famous with his original style in traditional Sámi music. In his music he mixes old joiking technics with modern electronic elements. Nature, animals and people are strongly present in his joiks.

 

Hilda Länsman is a young Sámi singer and song writer, who became known in Finland, when she took part in Eurovision qualification song contest UMK, with her mother Ulla Pirttijärvi-Länsman, in spring 2015.

 
Tickets: 25 € when bought in advance. 30 € on the day of the concert (sold one hour before the concert starts from Sajos Info).  

Tickets for the concert are available from Sámi Cultural Center Sajos, Inari, tel: +358 (0) 10 839 3168 or www.lipputoimisto.fi
