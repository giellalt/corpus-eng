Outdoor area

The grounds of the Sámi Cultural Centre are suitable for a range of open-air events, meeting the needs of the outdoor themes of fairs or of music festivals, for example. The Sámi Cultural Centre is located on the banks of the Juutuajoki river, in the heart of Inari village. Lake Inari lies only a few hundred metres away, allowing magnificent views of the lake from the grounds. Ample parking is available for the use of the various conferences and events.
