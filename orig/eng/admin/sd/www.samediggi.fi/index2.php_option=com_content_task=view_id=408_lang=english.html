Conference and Event Services

Conference and Event Services is responsible of marketing, sales and reservations for the centre’s common facilities, handling of the arrangements for events and conferences, and management of the centre’s technology, information and security services.

 

The Conference and Event Services office is located on the first floor of the cultural centre, adjacent to the lobby and conference rooms. More information about the reservations of the premises from here.
