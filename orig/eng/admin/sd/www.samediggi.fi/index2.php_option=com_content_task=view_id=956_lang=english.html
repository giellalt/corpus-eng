Sajos Club night on December 12th

Sajos Club night is celebrated at Sajos again with good music and relaxing atmosphere on Friday 12th of December starting at 8 pm. Come to listen great new Sami music as well as popmusic in the fourth club night at Sajos.

 

On the stage have already promised Out of the Woods, Aila-duo, Ravggon, Tiina Aikio and Tony Brantberg, Janne Lappalainen and Katri Kittilä, Ánnámáret Ensamble and  Groovenator. In addition, anyone who feels it, gets to climb on the stage and enchant the audience. Instruments of Sajos and the microfone are ready and waiting to be borrowed! When you get hungry, you may enjoy snacks and refreshments at restaurant Lyeme. Clubnight is hosted by Aslak Paltto and Heli Huovinen, familiar from the radio show Sohkaršohkka.

 

Ticket sale starts at Sajos at 8 pm and tickets for Club night cost 10 eur. Advance tickets are also available at Sajos info. Welcome!

 

Club night is organised by Sajos, Sámi Music Centre, Sámi Education Institute and Restaurant Lyeme.
