Former Minister of Labour and Social Inclusion Bjarne Håkon Hanssen (Labour Party)

(17.10.05–20.06.08 and 02.10.09–20.10.09)

Born: 1962

Curriculum Vitae for the Minister of Labour and Social Inclusion

Personal data

  • Family name: Hanssen
  • First names: Bjarne Håkon
  • Date of birth: 1 November 1962
  • Family: married, 4 children

Political Assignments

2008-2009 Minister of Health and Care Services and Minister of Labour and Social Inclusion

2005–2008:

 Minister of Labour and Social Inclusion

2003–2005:

 Head of the Labour Party’s Working Group for European Debate

2001–2005:

 Member of the Storting, the Norwegian Parliament, representing the county of Nord-Trøndelag. Member of the Standing Committee on Social Affairs

2001–2005:

 Member of the Labour Party’s Leading Group

2000–2001:

 Minister of Agriculture
1997–2001:

Member of the Storting, representing Nord-Trøndelag. Member of the Standing Committee on Business and Industry

1995–1997: Chairman of the Nord-Trøndelag City Council
1989–1993:

Alternate member of the Storting, Nord-Trøndelag

1980–1982: Executive Secretary, Nord-Trøndelag Labour Youth League
  

Professional Career 

1989–1991: Municipal Executive, Fosnes Municipality
1987–1989: Manager, Klingenbergheimen Centre for Mentally Disabled
  

Education

1985:

Teaching degree from Levanger College of Education

                  

 

  

Speeches and articles

  • Good work (24.01.2007)
  • Globalisation’s losers (03.09.2006)
  • A more inclusive labour market (07.07.2006)
  • Norwegian policies towards the 55 + group (23.05.2006)
  • Flexibility and security in a Norwegian Labour Market Perspective (24.01.2006)
