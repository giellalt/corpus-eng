Agriculture

Agricultural policy includes land management, agriculture and forestry, animal husbandry, reindeer husbandry and the development of new industries based on agriculture. The Government’s agricultural policy is intended to secure the resource basis for agricultural production, develop the knowledge base and contribute to value creation and employment in the whole country based on agriculture and agricultural production.


On this page you will find general information and documents relating to agriculture. You can explore this topic in more detail by using the menu on the right.
