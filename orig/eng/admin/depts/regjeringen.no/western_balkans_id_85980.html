Western Balkans Section

The general situation in the various Western Balkan countries and in the region as a whole. International cooperation and reforms with regard to the Western Balkans. Policy development and project administration in accordance with Norway’s general policy for assistance to the region (NOK 500 million in 2011). Following up regional cooperation organisations. Desk responsibility for Albania, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Croatia, Macedonia, Montenegro and Serbia (including Kosovo).

General director Haakon Blankenborg
