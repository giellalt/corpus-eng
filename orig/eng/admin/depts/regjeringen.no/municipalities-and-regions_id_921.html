Municipalities and regions

The Ministry of Local Government and Modernisation has overriding responsibility for matters relating to housing policy, regional policy, local administration and the holding of elections. 

On this page you will find general information and documents relating to the municipalities and regions. You can explore this topic in more detail by using the menu on the right.

Election Portal: valg.no

In 2015, there will be election to municipal and county councils in Norway. The election day is Monday 14 September 2015.
