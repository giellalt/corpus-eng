Press centre

The Communication Unit relays press enquiries to the political leadership. We help the press and the public to find background materials and information on ministerial matters. We are also responsible for the Ministry’s website.

Press phone +47 415 73 500

The communication policy of the Ministry of Petroleum and Energy is based on the main principles of the Freedom of Information Act and the Public Administration Act, which give the public the right of access to our work and our decisions. We attach importance to being open and accessible and providing good service to the media and others who contact us with enquiries. We are set up to pursue an active communication policy and require that the information we provide be correct, objective and reliable.

If you want to subscribe to press releases from the Ministry of Petroelum and Energy, send an e-mail to the communication unit,
