Law and order

The Ministry of Justice and the Police shall:

  • reduce and prevent crime
  • improve civil protection and emergency planning
  • contribute to expedient legislation that is adapted to societal developments
  • safeguard the due process of law and right to a fair trial
  • implement punishment whose goal is to return offenders to a normal life in community
  • prevent and resolve conflicts

On this page you will find general information and documents relating to law and order. You can explore this topic in more detail by using the menu on the right.
