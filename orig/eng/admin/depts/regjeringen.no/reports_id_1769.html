Health System in Transition, Norway

Health system review

[Ministry of Health and Care Services, 01.09.2014]

Norway’s fifth national report to the Convention on Biological Diversity

[Ministry of Climate and Environment, 10.07.2014]

The report presents information on status and trends for biodiversity in Norway, measures undertaken to implement the Convention since 2009, successes and remaining Challenges.

Barriers to digitizatioen

Executive summary of KPMG report to the Norwegian Ministry of Local Government and Modernisation

[Ministry of Local Government and Modernisation, 18.06.2014]

On the assignment from the Ministry of Local Government and Modernisation, KPMG has undertaken a mapping and analysis of barriers for digitalization among Norwegian Companies.

Norway’s sixth National Communication under the Framework Convention on Climate Change

Status report as of January 2014

[Ministry of Climate and Environment, 10.03.2014]

This report is Norway’s sixth national communication on national circumstances, polices and measures related to climate change under the Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC). The previous national communications were submitted in 1994, 1997, 2002, 2006 and 2010 respectively.

International migration 2012-2013 – Norway

[Ministry of Labour and Social Affairs, 10.01.2014]

Annually Norway sends a report to the OECD – Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development. The report “International Migration 2012-2013 – IMO report for Norway” is a contribution to the reporting system on migration for the OECD countries.

International migration 2012-2013 – Norway

[Ministry of Justice and Public Security, 06.01.2014]

Annually Norway sends a report to the OECD – Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development. The report “International Migration 2012-2013 – IMO report for Norway” is a contribution to the reporting system on migration for the OECD countries.

Internation Migration report 2012-2013

[Ministry of Children, Equality and Social Inclusion, 03.01.2014]

Annually Norway sends a report to the OECD – Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development. The report “International Migration 2012-2013 – IMO report for Norway” is a contribution to the reporting system on migration for the OECD countries.

Forecasting demand and supply of labour by education

[Ministry of Education and Research, 22.10.2013]

In Norway, supply of labour by level of education has developed in line with demand for many years. At the same time unemployment rates and relative wages for different educational groups have been quite stable over time.

Evaluation of Nordic World Heritage Foundation (2008-2013)

[Ministry of Climate and Environment, 16.10.2013]

På oppdrag fra Miljøverndepartementet og Unesco har Vista Analyse AS gjennomført en evaluering av Nordic World Heritage Foundation (NWHF). NWHF er et kategori 2-senter under beskyttelse av Unesco, som følge av en avtale mellom den norske regjeringen ved Miljøverndepartementet og Unesco. Avtalen er inngått for perioden 2008-2014.

Political platform

[The Office of the Prime Minister, 16.10.2013]

for a government formed by the Conservative Party and the Progress Party.
