Change of Government

Following the resignation tendered by Jens Stoltenberg’s second government 14 October, the King in a Council of State session at 10 am October 16th accepted the resignation as of 12.00 noon October 16th. At the same time, the King appointed Erna Solberg’s government to take over at 12 noon October 16th.

International health cooperation

Cooperating internationally on health issues serves many purposes, such as: contributing to solving the health challenges that do not stop at borders; exchanging knowledge; developing nationwide schemes for effectively preventing and combating disease; and, helping to improve the overall health situation in Norway’s vicinity and in other parts of the world.
