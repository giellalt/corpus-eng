Section for Central Europe and the EEA Financial Mechanisms

The EEA and Norway Grants: Budget and performance management, dialogue with the 15 Beneficiary States. Responsible for the Financial Mechanism Office in Brussels and coordination between the ministries and other actors in Norway. Desk responsibility for Bulgaria, the Czech Republic, Estonia, Hungary, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, Poland, Romania, Slovakia and Slovenia.

Director Mr Steinar Hagen.
