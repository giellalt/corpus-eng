Referrals 

Every year a large number of referrals are submitted to the  the County Administrative Board. This provides us the opportunity to comment on the proposals from different ministries, government agencies and others. 

The County Administrative Board sends many of its draft decisions for referral to the concerned stakeholders in the county, such as municipalities, government agencies and organizations.
