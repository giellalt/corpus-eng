Her Royal Highness Princess Ingrid Alexandra

Her Royal Highness Princess Ingrid Alexandra was born on 21 January 2004 at Rikshospitalet University Hospital in Oslo. The Princess is second in line of succession to the throne.

The Princess was christened in the Palace Chapel by Bishop Gunnar Stålsett on 17 April 2004.

Godparents

The Princess’s godparents are His Majesty The King, His Royal Highness Crown Prince Fredrik of Denmark, Her Royal Highness Crown Princess Victoria of Sweden, His Majesty King Felipe VI of Spain (then the Prince of Asturias), Her Highness Princess Märtha Louise, and Ms Marit Tjessem, the mother of the Crown Princess.

School

The Princess began attending day-care centre in Asker on 4 January 2006, and on 19 August 2010 she began attending Jansløkka elementary school. On 19 August 2014 Princess Ingrid Alexandra began attending Oslo International School at Bekkestua.

Official engagements

The Princess has already carried out several official engagements. She also normally joins other members of the Royal Family on the balcony of the Palace to greet the annual children’s parade on 17 May, Norwegian Constitution Day. She has also attended the traditional ski jumping tournament at Holmenkollen in Oslo.
