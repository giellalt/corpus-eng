Princess Ingrid Alexandra 6 years old

Today, 21 January 2010, Her Royal Highness Princess Ingrid Alexandra turned six years old. In honour of the occasion, the Royal Court has released eight new photos of the Princess.

21.01.2010

The pictures were taken earlier this week by photographer Jo Michael at the Crown Prince and Crown Princess' estate Skaugum.

For the press

High resolution handout photographs can be downloaded via the Photo album for the press-link. Please credit the photographer as well as The Royal Court, Norway.
