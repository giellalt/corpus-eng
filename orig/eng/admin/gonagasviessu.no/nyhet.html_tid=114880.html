Photos from Beitostølen

Their Royal Highnesses The Crown Prince and Crown Princess, Princess Ingrid Alexandra and Prince Sverre Magnus met the press for a family photo shoot at Beitsostølen today.

13.04.2013


The weather was marvellous at Beitostølen this afternoon. The family went skiing and sledging, and enjoyed a little rest with grilled marshmallows.

More pictures can be found in the photo albums via the link to the right.
